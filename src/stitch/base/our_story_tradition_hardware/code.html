THE CHRONICLE

A CENTURY OF PRECISION.

1924

The First Forge

Elias Thorne opens the Tradition Foundry in Sheffield, specializing in heavy-duty industrial contactors for the railway system.

1958

The Domestic Shift

Transitioning industrial reliability to the home. The first "Executive Lever" series is launched, setting the standard for architectural switches.

1990

Material Renaissance

Tradition Hardware introduces specialized ceramic and rare metal finishes, defying the global trend toward plastic components.

2024

The Silent Majority

In the age of IoT, we remain the definitive choice for those who value permanent, offline mechanical excellence.
